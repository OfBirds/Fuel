### What's new

- The unit picker now offers a fuller set of units — metric, imperial, and a few
  others — when you define a food, grouped so the common g / ml / piece stay on top.
  Prefer the simple list? Turn "Grouped unit picker" off in Settings → Display.
- The "Show macros" setting now works everywhere: protein, carbs, and fat fields
  stay hidden across the add, edit, and catalogue screens until you switch it on.
- AI-suggested food names are now shown in Title Case (e.g. "Scrambled Eggs on Toast").
