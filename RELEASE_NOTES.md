### What's new

- **We've moved — update your bookmark.** Indigo Swallow now lives at
  **https://swallow.bearsoft.duckdns.org**. The old address is going away, so please switch
  to the new one (and, if you installed the app, reinstall it from there).
- **New sign-in — re-register with the same email.** We're moving to a new authentication
  system. Please create your account again using **the same email address** you used before:
  do that and your existing food log, weigh-ins and profile stay linked and fully intact.
- **Fuel is now Indigo Swallow** — same app, new name and bird-mark; your data is untouched.
- **Install it like an app (PWA)** — add it to your home screen / desktop; it opens
  full-screen and loads instantly.
- **A clearer day view.** Your daily calories show as a progress ring against your goal,
  with calories-left as the headline number; the date navigation has been redesigned.
- **Edit the AI's suggestions — with undo.** Logging by text or photo, you can tweak the
  AI's amounts/calories/macros (changing the amount rescales the rest), and undo back to the
  AI's original suggestion in one tap.
- **Smoother number entry** — quantity/calorie/goal fields can be cleared and retyped without
  a stuck leading "0".
