### What's new in 1.7

- **Your data stays yours across sign-in changes.** Fuel now anchors your account to a
  stable internal id instead of the sign-in provider's id, so moving or upgrading the
  login system no longer risks orphaning your food log, weigh-ins or profile.

- **Clearer sign-in when your email isn't verified yet.** If you sign in before confirming
  your email and that address already has a Fuel account, we now **hold the sign-in and ask
  you to verify your email** — instead of dropping you into a blank account or a cryptic
  error. The moment you verify and sign in again, your existing data reconnects
  automatically. (Already landed in an empty account from an earlier sign-in? Just verify
  that email and sign in again — your data comes back, no support needed.)
