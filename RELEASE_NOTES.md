### What's new in 1.12

> **Note:** we're testing our rebuilt release-notification emails — that's why this update may
> land in your inbox again. It's a one-off; the next release will notify you normally.

This release makes the AI assistant look and behave the same everywhere, and irons out a
batch of rough edges you reported.

- **One AI panel everywhere.** Adding a food to the catalogue now uses the exact same
  **Text / Photo / Barcode** assistant as the diary — including barcode scanning, which the
  catalogue was missing. Describe it, snap it or scan it; the result now **fills the food form
  by itself** (no extra "apply" tap — Save Food is the only confirmation).

- **Taking photos is two clear buttons.** Wherever a photo is needed, you now get
  **"Take photo"** (opens the camera directly) and **"File upload"** (your gallery or files),
  side by side — no more guessing what one combined button will do on your device.

- **AI only where it helps.** The assistant appears when **adding** a food, not when editing
  one — editing an existing food is a manual correction, so the form opens clean.

- **Dialogs behave.** Pop-up dialogs are properly centered, follow your light/dark theme
  (no more black-on-dark text), and native dropdown lists now match the theme too.

- **Small clarity fixes.** Nutrition labels now read simply "Protein per 100 g" (the
  confusing "(g)" suffix is gone), and an ingredient's unit is shown as fixed — it comes from
  the food's own definition, same as when logging in the diary.

- **A tidier header.** Navigation now lives behind a proper **menu button** (☰) styled like
  its neighbours, with your account name shown inside the menu, above Logout.
